Savanna Parks Guide Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Sep 17th 2024

At Savanna Parks Guide, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). By using the Savanna Parks Guide App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information from users: Personal Information and Non-Personal Information.

Personal Information

Personal information is data that can be used to identify or contact you. The App may collect the following personal information when you interact with it:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, or phone number when you contact customer support or sign up for our newsletter.
  • Location Data: If you enable location services, we may collect your real-time geographic location to provide navigation services and nearby points of interest.
  • Device Information: Such as your device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for troubleshooting and improving our services.

Note: Location data is used solely for navigation and in-app purposes. You can opt-out of sharing location data at any time by disabling location services on your device.

Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information is anonymous or aggregated data that cannot be used to identify an individual. We collect the following types of non-personal information:

  • Usage Data: Information on how the app is accessed and used, including clicks, navigation patterns, and features used. This helps us understand user behavior and improve the app's design and performance.
  • Crash Reports and Diagnostics: Automatically generated reports when the app crashes or fails, which include device information but do not include personal identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the App: We use your data to deliver core app functions like navigation, offline maps, and personalized recommendations.
  • To Improve User Experience: Analyze how users interact with the app to refine features, design, and performance.
  • To Communicate with You: Send updates, respond to your inquiries, and provide customer support.
  • For Marketing and Promotions: With your consent, we may send you news, special offers, and promotions related to the parks or conservation efforts in Eastern Africa.
  • To Ensure App Security: Detect and protect against technical issues, fraud, or unauthorized access.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers to facilitate our services, such as hosting, analytics, and customer support. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information when required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our users, the public, or Savanna Parks Guide.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our business, your information may be transferred to the acquiring company.

4. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information. These may include:

  • Access: You have the right to request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You have the right to request correction of any inaccurate information about you.
  • Deletion: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data, subject to legal retention obligations.
  • Opt-Out: You may opt out of receiving marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in the emails or contacting us directly.
